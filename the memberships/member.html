According to the decision of the General Assembly of Zamalek Club, Zamalek club membership fees now amount to 180 thousand pounds for the high qualification, 310 thousand pounds for the average qualification, in addition to membership excluded at 22 thousand pounds for judges, members of the armed forces, police and journalists. The membership price of Zamalek club was raised in 2019 to 180 thousand pounds for the higher qualification instead of 62 thousand pounds, and 310 thousand pounds for the average qualification instead of 110 thousand pounds . Membership includes both husband and wife and children under 21 years of age. Children older than 21 years are separated from their father or mother for independent membership. Separation fees for a son or daughter are within LE 150-200 for the separation form. Membership as affiliate members with a fee of up to 150 pounds per person. The annual renewal of the full membership of 650 pounds, in addition to 200 pounds construction fees club branch in the city of October 6, while the annual renewal fees for membership of a member over 60 years only 186 pounds.
